// TANYA TALAGA: REWRITING CANADIAN HISTORY

EDITORIAL 2024 EDITORIAL LAYOUT

<Overview />

An editorial design project featuring the work and story of Tanya Talaga, exploring her process of uncovering family secrets and telling the unvarnished truth about Canada through her bestselling books.

<The Challenge />

The layout had to honour Talaga's journalism — restrained typography, generous white space, photography that's allowed to breathe — without ever turning her work into design fodder. The challenge was to make every page feel like an invitation to read rather than a graphic exercise, with hierarchy that lets her sentences land first.
