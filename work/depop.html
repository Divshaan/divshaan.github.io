// DEPOP: INTEGRATED CAMPAIGN

ADVERTISEMENT 2024 STUDENT BRIEF

<Overview />

An integrated campaign designed to "Make Depop the Talk of the Town" by bringing the online thrift store experience to real life. The campaign targets Gen Z thrift lovers with concepts like "Experience Your Online Store IRL" and "Thriftstore at Your Fingertips."

<The Challenge />

Depop lives almost entirely on a phone screen — its discovery loop, its community, its serendipity. The brief asked for a way to make that digital-native energy felt in physical space without losing the thrill of the scroll. The goal was an integrated system that translated app behaviour into out-of-home, print, and lifestyle moments Gen Z would actually stop for.
