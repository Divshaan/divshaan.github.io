// KHUSHMAN DHILLON LAW: WEBSITE DESIGN

WEB & UI 2025 CLIENT WORK

<Overview />

A professional website designed for Khushman Dhillon Law — Barrister & Solicitor based in Brampton, ON. The design emphasizes trust, clarity, and accessibility to effectively represent the firm's legal services. You can browse the live site below.

<The Challenge />

Legal practice sites tend to fall into one of two traps: stock-photo gloss or template anonymity. The brief asked for a site that signals competence on first scroll without becoming corporate wallpaper — clear practice areas, frictionless contact paths, and accessibility-conscious type and contrast for the firm's diverse client base.
